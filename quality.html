Our Commitment

At Ravi Seamless, quality is at the heart of every pipe and tube we produce. Following a strict "Zero Defect" policy, we ensure that our seamless pipes, stainless tubes, U-bend tubes, instrumentation tubes, and pipe fittings meet international ASTM/API standards. From material selection to final inspection, our advanced testing and quality management systems guarantee products you can trust in critical applications worldwide.

Advanced Testing

World-Class Laboratory

Equipped with modern NDT and destructive testing facilities to ensure zero-defect products.

01

Hydrostatic Testing

All seamless pipes and tubes undergo high-pressure water tests to verify leak-proof performance under extreme operating conditions.

02

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

Non-destructive evaluation to detect internal flaws, measure wall thickness, and ensure uniformity in stainless and carbon steel tubes.

03

Chemical Composition Analysis

Spectrometer tests confirm exact alloy composition to meet ASTM, API, and client-specific standards for every batch.

04

Mechanical Testing

Tensile, hardness, and impact tests certify the strength, elongation, and durability of pipes and tubes for high-pressure and critical applications.

05

Visual & Dimensional Inspection

100% visual and dimensional inspection ensures straightness, surface finish, and adherence to precise tolerances for all products.

06

Material Certification

Every shipment is accompanied by EN 10204 3.1 & 3.2 Material Test Certificates (MTC), providing full traceability and client assurance.
